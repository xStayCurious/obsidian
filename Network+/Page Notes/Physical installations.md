COMMON EXAM TRAPS
-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking PoE only carries power.

Wrong.

PoE carries:
- power
and:
- network data

-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking UPS systems provide indefinite power.

Wrong.

UPS systems provide:
- temporary backup power

-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking heat only affects performance.

Wrong.

Heat can:
- physically damage equipment

-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking humidity only affects comfort.

Wrong.

Humidity affects:
- ESD risk
- condensation risk

-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking EMI and RFI are identical.

Wrong.

RFI is a type of electromagnetic interference specifically involving radio frequencies.

-----------------------------------

Trap:
Thinking physical security is unrelated to networking.

Wrong.

Physical compromise can lead to:
- network compromise
- outages
- theft
- tampering
